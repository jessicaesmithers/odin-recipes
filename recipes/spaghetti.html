Spaghetti

Because everyone knows that homemade spaghetti is the best.

Sometimes simple is best. Why throw in everything and the kitchen sink, when all you need is noodles, sauce, and meat?

Ingredients:

  • 1 jar of Ragu (flavored with meat)
  • 1 box of angel hair noodles
  • 1 pound of fresh ground beef
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 jar of grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat a large pot of water until boiling
  2. Add noodles and return to boil
  3. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until desired tenderness
  4. Drain noodles
  5. Brown beef in a skillet
  6. Combine beef and sauce in a large pot
  7. Cook on a medium-high heat until boiling
  8. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
  9. Combine noodles and sauce at the desired ratio on each plate
  10. Enjoy!
