Tuna Casserole

A Bernard family favorite since 1979

This delicious blend of Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup, Ruffles potato chips, milk, and tuna is sure be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 2/3 cups whole milk
  • 1 bag potato chips
  • 1 bag of medium egg noodles
  • 1 can of tuna

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Heat a large pot of water until boiling
  3. Add noodles and return to boil
  4. Cook for 7-8 minutes or until desired tenderness
  5. Drain noodles
  6. Add chips to blender and blend until a mix of fine crumbs is formed, then remove chips and set aside
  7. Combine milk and soup in a blender and blend until all mushroom chunks are eliminated
  8. Combine cooked noodles, sauce mixture, and tuna in a casserole dish
  9. Sprinkle crushed chips on top of casserole
  10. Cook in oven for 17 minutes
  11. Remove from oven and enjoy
